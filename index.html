About Bibek Patra

Entrepreneur, developer, author & technology creator from Mayurbhanj, Odisha, India.

Bibek Patra is an entrepreneur, developer, author and technology creator from Mayurbhanj, Odisha, India. He is the founder of multiple digital ventures including BibekBook, Gengram, Vivika AI and Krayvo. His work focuses on publishing technology, artificial intelligence, social networking platforms, e-commerce systems, web development, digital marketing and 3D design. With experience in business development, content creation, branding, marketing and software development, he aims to create impactful digital products that improve people's lives.
