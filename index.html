📋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an author profile? +
Click on "Publish Your" in the menu, fill in your details, and submit. You'll need a unique username and a valid UPI ID.
How much does it cost to publish a book? +
Publishing on Bibek Books is completely free. You only pay a small commission when you make a sale.
How do I get paid for my book sales? +
Payments are sent directly to your registered UPI ID. You'll receive your earnings after each successful sale.
Can I edit my book after publishing? +
Yes, you can edit your book anytime. Go to your dashboard, find your book, and click the edit button.
What formats are supported for books? +
Currently, we support PDF files. You can upload your PDF and readers will be able to download or read it online.
Is there a limit to how many books I can publish? +
No, you can publish as many books as you like. There's no limit for verified authors.
